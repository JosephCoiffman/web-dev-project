Background

The path to the second Champion was very unlikely. The Miami Heat (Pat Riley), managed to create the "Big Three". The team was a super team like no other, With Lebron James Dwyane Wade and Chris Bosh. But it wasn't all fun and games. In just the previous season, the 2011 season, the Heat were embarrased by the Dallas Mavs and evryone was saying Lebron cannot win a champoinship.

The Miami heat then went on to beat the Thunder in a gentlemans sweep in 5 games. The next season.
